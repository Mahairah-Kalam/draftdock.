Get Started
Get to work,with a lot less work.
DraftDock delivers tools that improve your work and let you collaborate with ease, all with best-in-class security to keep it safe.


With DraftDock you can edit and sign your documents, collaborate on projects, search across all your apps, and it happens in the same place you securely store all your content. It’s that simple :)



Documents Analytics

Instant, Actionable Insights

With DraftDock DocSend, page-by-page analytics and permission controls track file use in real-time, so you can see when clients, contractors, or collaborators have viewed your content.






Content Collaboration

Work with anyone, from anywhere, on any device

DraftDock delivers flexible sharing, smart organization, and seamless collaboration—even for those without an account—so you and your team can get it done, and done right.














Security never comes second
From industry-leading encryption and tamper-proof documents to version history and recovery, DraftDock keeps your intellectual property safe and never sells or shares your data.
